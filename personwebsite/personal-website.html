About us

Who are we?

The idea shop was started back in 2000. The idea of this business was to bring and help out the young people. There is so much talent that young people tend to waste because they have no one to guide them and help them make positive change in their lives.because of the taste of the fahion and style they were attracted and desire to have, I saw the opportunity to come in and help them with their ideas and desires. They were creative on how they wear their clothes and how they look in style of their own.As a results some other kids wanted the same style but it's the style and fashion that is not available. So I decided to start something that will be of help to young people. I went to schools looking for the talented young fashionist and groomed them and gave them the opportunity to develop their talent and actually create it and sew it, so that everyone could get it. As a results we have achieved alot and the aim is to be known and seen as the world's in-demand fashionist. We are young, creative and best at bringing change with new ideas of styles in the fashion business.
